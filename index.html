WURLD

A proof of concept for an environmental impact app that allows users to track their positive contributions to the environment and to engage in friendly competition with others through gamification. WURLD seeks to create a community of like-minded individuals who help and inspire each other to make environmentally-conscious decisions. This app was built using a VueJS frontend, a Rails backend, and a PostGresSQL database.
